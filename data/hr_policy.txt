HR POLICY DOCUMENT - CONFIDENTIAL
Department: Human Resources

Leave Policy:
- Employees get 20 paid leaves per year
- Sick leave: 10 days per year
- Maternity leave: 6 months paid
- Paternity leave: 2 weeks paid

Salary:
- Salaries are credited on the 1st of every month
- Appraisals happen every April
- Employee payroll is strictly confidential

Code of Conduct:
- Employees must maintain professional behavior
- Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited
- Work hours: 9am to 6pm Monday to Friday
